Automated Content & Project Management System in Notion

NotionZapierProcess AutomationCRM

Tools Used: Notion, Zapier

My Role: Automation Specialist

The Challenge

The client faced the challenge of efficiently organizing his content data, making it difficult to easily find and manage specific information. Concurrently, the manual process of posting content was time-consuming, consuming valuable resources that could be better spent elsewhere.

The Solution

My solution involved building an intuitive Notion workspace with filters for easy content organization and review. A dedicated posting section provided oversight of new, review-ready, and published content, including integrated form requests. Crucially, the posting process was automated: new Notion items triggered automatic Ghost draft creation, allowing the client to simply review and publish

The Result

The client now has a brilliantly organized content hub that makes finding and managing data effortless. The automated posting process significantly cut down on manual work, saving him valuable time and ensuring content goes live smoothly.
